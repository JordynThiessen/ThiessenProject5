Information About Me

Hello my name is Jordyn Thiessen and I am currently a Junior at Northwest Missouri State University. I am a Interactive Digital Media Major in the Visual Imaging branch. I am creating this website for my Web Development I course, I am sharing a hobby of mine that I started doing during Quarantine during this Pandemic. This hobby is piecing together Metal Models that come together as miniture scluptures. The brand I put together is Metal Earth and they have loads of models you could comeplete yourself.

I have many hobbies such as reading, painting and traveling. But metal models have become a favorite of mine, they are fun and easy for me to complete. Then when you're done you have something cool to show people.

This site was created as part of the Web Development I course at Northwest Missouri State University and was developed to learn and enhance the concepts within that course. It is not intended for
public use.
